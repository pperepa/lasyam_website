Curriculum

Beginner (Level 1)

This level introduces students to the traditional classical dance technique of Kuchipudi. Provides an introduction to theory and practicals.
  • Basic Foot Steps (Adavus)
  • Advanced Steps
  • Jathis
  • Recitation (Slokas)
  • Basic Hand Gestures

Intermediate (Level 2)

This level is offered to students as an introduction to Abhinayam. In this level, we also teach the basics of the theory Natyothpatthiand the origin of Kuchipudi Natyam based on Natyasastram and Abhinayadarpanam. A detailed study of the mudras is covered as well.
  • Puja Nrityam
  • Sabdham
  • Jatiswaram
  • Keerthana
  • Neck, eye, and eyebrow movements (Greeva, Dristi, & Bru Bedhas)
  • Paadha Bedhas

Advanced (Level 3)

This level introduces advanced theory as part of the complete study of Dasvathara, Navagraha Hastas, Navarasas, Nayika Nayaka Bedhas, and Asha Vidha Nayikas and their usage in Kuchipudi.
  • Tarangam
  • Bhamakalapam
  • Thillana
  • Slokam
  • Astapadi
  • Padam or Javali

Choreography & Nattuvangam (Level 4)

This level introduces choreography and the Tala System (nattuvangam). Introduction to the Tala system and music. Deeper study of Natyasastram and techniques of Abhinayam. Students are required to complete projects in choreography.
  • Slokam
  • Astapadi
  • Padam or Javali
